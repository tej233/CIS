Video Editing


About Video Editing



Video Editing Courses helps students garner skills to use different tools, softwares and fundamentals of video editing. It helps to improve or enhance the video quality for better experience for the audience. Video Editing Courses make aspirants familiar with the editing and assembly of recorded raw material into a finished product for broadcasting. In this course, students are taught cutting and editing videos through various hands-on practical training. Video Editing Courses are available at Diploma, Bachelor, Master and Certification levels. Screen Direction, Sound Design, Continuity, Titling, Picture Management, Color Correction and Special Effects are some of the concepts that are covered in video editing courses. Video editing can be learnt separately or students also have the option of learning about video editing in other courses such as Mass communication Course or Film Making courses.
